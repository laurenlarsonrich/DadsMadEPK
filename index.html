Dad's Mad progressed from friends in a high school band to recoding their first song late one evening in the basement. Their song, “Queen Bee,” was forwarded to Shawn Black, the lead singer in Five Below Band. He liked what he heard and offered them their first gig on a professional stage in front of a sold out crowd. The band came to life on stage and the crowd wanted more. They loved performing and it showed.

Dad's Mad is a new alternative rock sound with unique instruments including the ukulele and trombone. Each band member plays several instruments which allows for a wider expression of their instrumental creativity. Their music comes together in a youthful fusion that is appealing to all ages of listeners.

Nate and Ben have family in two well known Lexington bands, The Five Below Band and The Twigginburys. This musical support has been a driving factor for their start and a motivation for success.

Dad's Mad came in third in their first Battle of The Bands competition at Riverfestivus. They have performed at Manchester Music Hall, Oktoberfest in Harrodsburg, Banners, The Celler, Brick Oven, Greyline Station, Bardstown Arts & Crafts Fair, Proud Mary BBQ, and Wildside Winery.
